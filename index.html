മലയാളം
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
1
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تُقَدِّمُوا۟ بَيْنَ يَدَىِ ٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ
O you who have believed, do not put yourselves before Allah and His Messenger, but fear Allah. Indeed, Allah is Hearing and Knowing.
2
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تَرْفَعُوٓا۟ أَصْوَٰتَكُمْ فَوْقَ صَوْتِ ٱلنَّبِىِّ
O you who have believed, do not raise your voices above the voice of the Prophet or be loud to him in speech like the loudness of some of you to others, lest your deeds become worthless while you perceive not.
3
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يَغُضُّونَ أَصْوَٰتَهُمْ عِندَ رَسُولِ ٱللَّهِ
Indeed, those who lower their voices before the Messenger of Allah — they are the ones whose hearts Allah has tested for righteousness. For them is forgiveness and a great reward.
4
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يُنَادُونَكَ مِن وَرَآءِ ٱلْحُجُرَٰتِ
Indeed, those who call out to you from behind the chambers — most of them do not use reason.
5
وَلَوْ أَنَّهُمْ صَبَرُوا۟ حَتَّىٰ تَخْرُجَ إِلَيْهِمْ
And if they had been patient until you could come out to them, it would have been better for them. But Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.
6
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ إِن جَآءَكُمْ فَاسِقٌۢ بِنَبَإٍ فَتَبَيَّنُوٓا۟
O you who have believed, if there comes to you a disobedient one with information, investigate, lest you harm a people out of ignorance and become regretful over what you have done.
1
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تُقَدِّمُوا۟ بَيْنَ يَدَىِ ٱللَّهِ
സത്യവിശ്വാസികളേ, അല്ലാഹുവിന്റെയും അവന്റെ ദൂതന്റെയും മുമ്പില്‍ നിങ്ങള്‍ മുന്‍കടക്കരുത്. നിങ്ങള്‍ അല്ലാഹുവെ ഭയപ്പെടുക. നിശ്ചയം, അല്ലാഹു സര്‍വ്വകേള്‍വിക്കാരനും സര്‍വ്വജ്ഞനുമാകുന്നു.
2
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تَرْفَعُوٓا۟ أَصْوَٰتَكُمْ
സത്യവിശ്വാസികളേ, നബിയുടെ സ്വരത്തിന് മുകളില്‍ നിങ്ങളുടെ ശബ്ദം ഉയര്‍ത്തരുത്. നിങ്ങള്‍ അറിയാതെ നിങ്ങളുടെ കര്‍മ്മങ്ങള്‍ നിഷ്ഫലമായിപ്പോകുമോ എന്ന് ഭയന്ന്.
3
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يَغُضُّونَ أَصْوَٰتَهُمْ
നിശ്ചയം, അല്ലാഹുവിന്റെ ദൂതന്റെ അടുക്കല്‍ ശബ്ദം കുറക്കുന്നവര്‍, ആ ആളുകളുടെ ഹൃദയങ്ങളെ അല്ലാഹു ഭക്തിക്കായി പരിശോധിച്ചിരിക്കുന്നു. അവര്‍ക്ക് പൊറുതിയും വലിയ പ്രതിഫലവുമുണ്ട്.
4
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يُنَادُونَكَ مِن وَرَآءِ ٱلْحُجُرَٰتِ
നിശ്ചയം, മുറിക്ക് പിന്നില്‍ നിന്ന് നിന്നെ വിളിക്കുന്നവരില്‍ അധിക പേരും ബോധമില്ലാത്തവരാണ്.
TAFSIR IBN KATHIR
سُورَةُ الحُجُرَات
Al-Hujurat · 18 Verses · Madinah
Verse 1 · يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟...
Allah commands the believers to submit to His commands and those of His Messenger ﷺ, and not to rush into decisions or actions before receiving divine guidance. Ibn Kathir explains that this verse was revealed when some companions spoke before the Prophet ﷺ could respond, teaching the etiquette of patience and reverence before the Messenger of Allah.
Al-Bukhari recorded that this verse was revealed regarding Abu Bakr and Umar (may Allah be pleased with them), who raised their voices during negotiations. They quickly corrected themselves upon its revelation.
Verse 2 · لَا تَرْفَعُوٓا۟ أَصْوَٰتَكُمْ...
This verse establishes the supreme etiquette of respecting the Prophet ﷺ. Ibn Kathir states that raising one's voice above the Prophet's voice was a potential cause for deeds to be nullified. The companions took this so seriously that Thabit ibn Qays, who had a naturally loud voice, feared he was among those condemned.
Ibn Abi Hatim recorded: When this verse was revealed, Thabit ibn Qays sat in his home in grief. The Prophet ﷺ consoled him: "You are not of them, you will live a praiseworthy life and die a martyr's death."
Verse 6 · إِن جَآءَكُمْ فَاسِقٌۢ بِنَبَإٍ...
This verse establishes one of Islam's foundational principles of justice: verification of news before acting upon it. It was revealed regarding Al-Walid ibn Uqbah, whom the Prophet ﷺ sent to collect Zakat. It is considered a cornerstone of Islamic epistemology — every piece of news must be investigated before action is taken.
